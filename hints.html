Tip #1 The most important thing to remember in Diablo is to understand your build and focus on what your build will need or currently needs in order to get past the more trying quests. Moreover, it’s crucial to allocate your skill points to the right slots. Here’s more on everything in this beginner’s guide you can do to enhance your gameplay and experience. Tip #2 The Warrior is the first character every newbie should consider starting off with. The Warrior is the best survivor early on in the game and is decent throughout the game. The warrior doesn't have to worry about spells very much. The only spells they really use the most are town portal spell and fire wall.
